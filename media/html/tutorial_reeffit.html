   Tutorial: REEFFIT


REEFFIT is a method to fit RNA secondary structure ensembles to multi-dimensional chemical mapping data. It is useful in scenarios where the probed RNA is suspected to fold into multiple states that are stabilized through perturbations (such as mutations in a mutate-and-map experiment). REEFFIT will attempt to come up with a secondary structure ensemble de novo and set their weights to best model the data.

Submitting entries to the RMDB

New experimental data can be uploaded to the RMDB as a spreadsheet in the SNRNASM Isa-Tab format or in RDAT file format. Public release of the data requires passing review by the RMDB team; the data must include error estimates, information on estimated or known structure (at least at the level of secondary structure), and description of how the data were processed.
